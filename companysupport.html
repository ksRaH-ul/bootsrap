How can I check my data balance and validity?
You can check your data balance by dialing *123# or using our mobile app. Simply open the app, go to the “My Account” section, and your remaining data and validity will be displayed on the home screen.
How do I activate an international roaming pack?
You can activate international roaming through our mobile app, website, or by sending an SMS with the keyword “IR” to 12345. Make sure to activate the pack before you travel to avoid extra charges.
What should I do if I lose my SIM card?
If your SIM card is lost or stolen, immediately call our customer care or visit the nearest service center to block your number and get a replacement SIM. This helps protect your account and personal information.
